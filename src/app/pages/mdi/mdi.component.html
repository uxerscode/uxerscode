Material Design Icons

Coming soon, the Material Design Icons plugin for Adobe XD.

The plugin is currently being reviewed by Adobe. When available, you can download from the Adobe XD plugin manager.

Enter the search criteria and press [enter] or click the search button.
Click on the icon you would like to add to your document, and the icon will be added center of the current document.

To report problems, please use our github page, or email us at uxerscode@gmail.com.
