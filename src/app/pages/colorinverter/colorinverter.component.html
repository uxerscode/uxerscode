Color Inverter

Introducing the Color Inverter plugin for Adobe XD.

Plugin Install

At present, color inverter only inverts solid colors, and gradients are not affected.

To invert a specific item or group of items, select them and then click on the "Color Inverter" from the plugin menu, or press "Ctrl+Shift+Alt+I" on Windows or "Cmd+Shift+Alt+I" on Mac to invert.
To invert the entire document (including color assets) invoke the plugin as above without a selection.
